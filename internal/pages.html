Virtus AI Factory

AI · Manufacturing
/products/virtus-ai-factory

Pitch: AI assistant that supercharges MES/MOM development and daily operations on Delmia Apriso, Siemens Opcenter, and other major MOM platforms.

  • Hero — two-mode value: "For developers" + "For shop-floor users"
  • Developer mode — code-aware assistant for Apriso/Opcenter customization, config, BPM, scripting
  • User mode — natural-language operator support, work-instruction Q&A, alarm triage
  • Supported platforms — Delmia Apriso · Siemens Opcenter · others (list)
  • How it integrates — connectors, on-prem option, data residency
  • Case study or scenario walkthrough
