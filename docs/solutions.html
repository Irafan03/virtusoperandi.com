Bundled outcomes

Solutions, not modules.

When you know the outcome but not the stack: three pre-composed solutions that bundle products around a goal you can name.

AI-Powered Smart Factory

For tier-1 and tier-2 manufacturers running Delmia Apriso, Siemens Opcenter, or another MOM platform. Cut customization time from quarters to weeks, automate the workflows your MES doesn't cover, and make QMS documents findable.

Includes: Virtus AI Factory + Virtus Agents + Virtus GED.

Typical outcome: 9-month customization → 3 weeks. Operator adoption stops being a battle.

Logistics Command Center

For freight carriers, last-mile operators, and fleet-rental businesses. Replace the spreadsheets-and-WhatsApp stack with a real dispatch board, a driver app, and back-office automation that closes the paper-to-finance loop.

Includes: Virtus Fleet + Virtus ERP + Virtus Lever.

Typical outcome: Zero paper between driver and finance. Dispatchers stop opening spreadsheets within three weeks.

Office Automation Suite

For consulting, professional services, and back-office teams. Hours back per knowledge worker, faster proposal cycles, fewer dropped follow-ups — without forcing anyone onto a new platform.

Includes: Virtus Lever + Virtus Agents + Virtus GED.

Typical outcome: 6 hours/week back per knowledge worker. Proposal cycles 40% shorter.

Solutions are starting points, not lock-ins. Drop a module, add another, scale up — the catalogue composes to the shape you need.
