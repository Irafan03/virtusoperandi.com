Frequently asked questions.

Everything you need to know before you start.

Can I switch plans at any time?+

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately on a pro-rata basis. Downgrades apply at the start of the next billing period.

What is the Founding plan?+

Early access at a price-locked rate for the first users. The price will never increase as long as the subscription stays active. Limited seats.

How does annual billing work?+

Pay 12 months upfront and save ~20% compared to the monthly rate. The discount applies to Pro and Power plans only.

Are prices inclusive or exclusive of tax?+

All displayed prices are ex-tax. Applicable VAT is added at checkout based on your country of residence.

What does Team / Enterprise include?+

On-prem or dedicated cloud deployment, guaranteed SLA, guided onboarding, custom integrations, and a dedicated account manager. Contact us for a quote within 24 h.
