How we operate

Operandi. The way of operating.

We codify what already works in great operations into software — instead of forcing your team to change how they work.

01 / Method

Configure, don't customize.

Every product ships with sensible defaults and config-level levers. No bespoke forks, no consultant lock-in, no six-month customizations that age out in a year.

02 / Method

Connect, don't replace.

Apriso, Opcenter, Microsoft 365, Salesforce, Gmail — we integrate with the systems your team already runs on. Adopt one product at a time, keep what works.

03 / Method

Compose, don't fork.

Six products, one platform. Pick the modules you need now, add the rest when the business asks for them. Every product speaks the same data model.

Ready to put it to work?

Fifteen minutes. A real engineer on the call. We'll walk through your operation and show you exactly which products fit — and which don't.
