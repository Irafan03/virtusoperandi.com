Cookie Policy.

Cookies and similar technologies we use on virtusoperandi.com, what they do, and how to control them. Last reviewed 2026-06-08.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, to make them work more efficiently, and to provide information to the owners of the site. We use cookies along with similar technologies (local storage, pixels) for the purposes described below.

Cookies we use

CookiePurposeCategoryLifetime
vo_session Keeps you signed in across pages. Essential Session
vo_csrf Protects forms from cross-site request forgery. Essential Session
vo_consent Remembers your cookie consent choice. Essential 12 months
vo_pref Remembers UI preferences (theme, language). Functional 12 months
_plausible_id* Anonymous usage analytics (no cross-site tracking). Analytics 24 hours

Essential cookies are required for the site to function and cannot be disabled. Functional and analytics cookies are only set after you accept them via the consent banner.

Managing your preferences

You can change your cookie preferences at any time:

  • Use the cookie banner shown on your first visit, or reopen it from the footer (link to be added).
  • Configure your browser to block or delete cookies. Most browsers offer this in settings — note that blocking essential cookies will break parts of the site.

Third-party services

We currently use the following third-party services that may set cookies:

  • Plausible Analytics — privacy-friendly, cookieless by default.
  • Cloudflare — bot mitigation and CDN; sets essential cookies for security.

We do not use cookies for cross-site advertising or behavioral profiling.

Updates to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as we add or remove tooling. Material changes will be communicated via the site at least thirty (30) days before they take effect.
